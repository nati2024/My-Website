All About Me!

Interests and Abilities

Posted by Natalie Kearl on December 07, 2021

Hi! My name is Natalie Kearl, I grew up in the golden state of California before moving to Utah to continue my edducations at BYU. I am the oldest of four girls so I am no stranger to chaotic situations! I am currently a student at BYU and I hope that one day I can utilize my skills in ways that will more positively impact the world around me. I am passionate about empowering the people around me and changing the world for the better in my own small way.

I love going on long runs as the sun rises or as the sun sets. It's challenging for sure but the amount of satisfaction that I get having completed something that I knew would be difficult for me makes it all worth it! My mom is an immigrant from Venezuela so I am also capable of conversational spanish and grew up knowing the value in hard work because of my mom and her example. I love cartoons, comedies, and memes because there should be more laughter in everyone's lives! Even if there's something that I am struggling with I will learn to get it right through practice and teaching because I am very determined to improve.

It is through the hardships of my family and the variety of jobs that I have worked that I have been able to transform into the person I am today. Although some things aren't meant to last forever I always find something to take away from my experiences to be grateful for. My whole life I have admired women with the capacity to improve not just their own lives but the lives of those around them. I hope that one day I too can empower others the way that they empowered me.
